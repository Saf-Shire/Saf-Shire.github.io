For Business



Welcome to Unconscious Data Bias - a tool designed to help you measure and improve gender diversity within your company!

Unconscious Data Bias analyses your employee demographic data in accordance with GDPR, and produces a visualisation tailored to your organisation. Locate diversity problem areas in your workforce within a matter of minutes!

Studies show that having a diverse workforce improves the success of an organisation. Better representation = better results! Get in touch today to begin your diversity journey.
